     INFO



            Agro Digi is an initiative to link agriculture with the digital world. Digital India is a campaign launched by the Government of India to ensure that Government services are made available to citizens electronically by improved online infrastructure and by increasing Internet connectivity or by making the country digitally empowered in the field of technology. Agro Digi is a small step in the same direction. Agriculture and allied sectors like forestry and fisheries accounted for 13.7% of the GDP (gross domestic product) and about 50% of the workforce. This website along with providing a digital platform for agriculture also makes an effort to bring about some changes in various fields of agriculture like buying and selling stuff, waste management, supplements, etc. Also a little digital input in agriculture will definitely attract the upcoming generation to make active participation in it as the economic contribution of agriculture to India's GDP is steadily declining with the country's broad-based economic growth.
